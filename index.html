Our Story

Established in 2000, Adishakti Agro Industries LLP began as a small family-owned cashew processing unit with a vision to deliver premium quality cashews across pan-India. What started as a modest operation has now grown into one of the leading cashew processors in the country.

Our journey has been marked by a commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainable practices. Over the years, we have invested in state-of-the-art processing facilities, implemented rigorous quality control measures, and established strong relationships with farmers to ensure a consistent supply of the finest cashews.

Today, Adishakti Agro Industries LLP is proud to be a trusted name in the cashew industry, supplying our products to various regions across India while also exporting to key international markets, including the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, and Japan.
